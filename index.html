How I Can Help

Services Offered

Humanistic | Person Centred Therapy

Humanistic and person centred therapy focuses on you as a whole person, supporting your natural capacity for growth and change. It offers a safe, non-judgemental, empathic space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings. This approach can support issues including low mood, anxiety, depression, relationship challenges, and life transitions including menopause, grief, and loss.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) is a practical approach that looks at how your thoughts, feelings, and behaviours are connected. CBT offers tools to manage unhelpful patterns and develop healthier ways of thinking and responding. It is often used for anxiety, depression, and stress.

Counselling

Counselling offers a confidential, one-to-one space where you can speak openly and honestly about whatever you are carrying. Whether you are navigating a difficult period, feeling overwhelmed, or simply struggling to make sense of your emotions, counselling provides a calm and non-judgemental environment to explore what is on your mind.

Psychotherapy

Psychotherapy is a deeper, longer-term therapeutic process that invites you to explore the underlying patterns, beliefs, and past experiences that shape how you think, feel, and relate to others. Rather than focusing solely on immediate concerns, psychotherapy looks beneath the surface to help you understand yourself more fully.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Psychodynamic therapy explores how past experiences and unconscious patterns shape your thoughts, feelings, and relationships today. By developing insight, it can help you understand yourself more fully and create lasting change.

Behavioural Dependencies

Behavioural dependencies can develop when certain habits become a way of coping with difficult emotions or life pressures. Therapy offers a non-judgemental space to explore what lies beneath these patterns and find healthier ways of coping.
