A therapist who truly listens.

I'm a fully accredited counsellor and psychotherapist with years of experience helping individuals navigate anxiety, depression, grief, relationship difficulties, and life transitions.

My approach is person-centred at its core — which means I believe deeply in your innate capacity to grow and heal. I draw from a range of therapeutic approaches to tailor our work specifically to you and your needs.

Sessions are available both in-person in Donegal and online, making support accessible no matter where you are or what your schedule looks like.

Why Choose Therapy with Me?

Tailored to You

No two journeys are the same. I take the time to truly understand your experiences and work with you to build a therapeutic approach that fits your individual needs, pace, and goals.

A Safe Space to Be Heard

Sometimes all we need is somewhere to speak freely. I offer a warm, completely confidential environment where you can share what's on your mind without fear of judgement.

Support That Goes at Your Pace

Healing isn't linear and there's no rush. Whether you're taking your first step or continuing a longer journey, I'll be with you every step of the way — at whatever pace feels right for you.

How I Can Help

Services Offered

Counselling

A supportive, one-to-one space where you can talk through whatever is weighing on you. Counselling helps you gain clarity, process difficult emotions, and develop healthier ways of coping with life's challenges.

Psychotherapy

Longer-term therapeutic work that explores the deeper patterns, beliefs, and experiences shaping how you think and feel. Psychotherapy offers lasting insight and meaningful, sustainable change.

Grief & Loss

Losing someone or something important can leave you feeling lost, overwhelmed, or alone. I provide a compassionate space to grieve at your own pace and find a way forward that honours your loss.

Trauma

Trauma can leave deep marks that affect every area of your life. In a safe and carefully paced environment, I'll support you in processing past experiences and reclaiming a sense of safety and calm.

Anxiety

Anxiety can be exhausting — the constant worry, the racing thoughts, the feeling that something is always wrong. Together we'll work to understand what's driving it and build practical tools to help you feel more grounded.

Lack of Direction

Feeling stuck, unfulfilled, or unsure of where you're heading is more common than you might think. Therapy can help you reconnect with your values, rediscover your sense of purpose, and move forward with confidence.
