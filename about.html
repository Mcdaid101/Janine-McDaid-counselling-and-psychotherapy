About Me

Janine Mc Daid

I am a qualified counsellor and Psychotherapist with an Honours Bachelor of Science degree in Counselling and Psychotherapy awarded by PCI College and the University of Middlesex. I am a pre-accredited member of the Irish Association of Counsellors and Psychotherapists (IACP) and have extensive experience supporting clients with a wide range of issues.

As a mother of four, I bring both professional training and lived experience into my work. This has deepened my empathy, patience, and understanding of the challenges individuals and families can face.

I am deeply passionate about my role as a counsellor and psychotherapist and I aim to offer a safe compassionate, empathetic and confidential space where you can feel heard, supported and understood.

Sessions are available both in-person in Donegal and online.

How I Can Support You

I offer a safe, supportive, and confidential space where you can explore what you're going through at your own pace.

Below is a list of just some of the problems I can support you with.

  • • Anxiety and overwhelm
  • • Stress and burnout
  • • Depression and low mood
  • • Grief, loss and bereavement
  • • Relationship challenges
  • • Family Issues
  • • Personal growth
  • • Confidence and low self-esteem
  • • Trauma and emotional difficulties
  • • Life transitions eg. menopause, career changes
  • • Feeling stuck, lack of direction
  • • Behavioral dependencies

All enquiries are completely confidential. Sessions are available in person in Donegal and online.
